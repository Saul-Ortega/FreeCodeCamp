History

The story of Java Grains begins in the heart of an island called by the same name located in Indonesia. It was brung from Ethiopia by Dutch because the island offered optimal conditions for it's growth. They started growing Arabica coffee and then continued with Robusta and Liberica due to a plage in 1880.

How It's Made

The plant could be cultivated both in the shade and in the sun once it's done, we wait between 3-4 years to produce the first harvesting, then the plant could last for about 20 years.

We do the picking hand-made to don't damage the plant and we leave the ones that are still green.

After that, the grains are exposed to hot air and humidity to modify the color and flavour.

To finish, the grains are baked and packed ready for it's shipment.
